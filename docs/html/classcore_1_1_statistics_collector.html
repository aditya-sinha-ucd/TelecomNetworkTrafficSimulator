Detailed Description

Immutable-interval statistics sink for the simulator.

Receives (time, rate) tuples, keeps them in-memory, and offers reporting utilities suited for visualization or downstream analysis. Inputs: timestamps and aggregate rates from core.Simulator. Outputs: summary metrics, Hurst estimates, CSV/text exports.
