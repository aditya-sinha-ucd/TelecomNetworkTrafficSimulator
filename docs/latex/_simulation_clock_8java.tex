\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Shared notion of simulation time across the core subsystems. 

The clock is advanced monotonically by {\ttfamily \doxylink{classcore_1_1_simulator}{core.\+Simulator}} as it processes {\ttfamily \doxylink{classcore_1_1_event}{core.\+Event}} objects from the {\ttfamily \doxylink{classcore_1_1_event_queue}{core.\+Event\+Queue}}. Other collaborators such as {\ttfamily \doxylink{classcore_1_1_statistics_collector}{core.\+Statistics\+Collector}} consult the clock to compute durations, rates, and time-\/weighted averages.
