\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Captures time-\/series samples and derives summary metrics. 

The collector is fed by {\ttfamily \doxylink{classcore_1_1_simulator}{core.\+Simulator}} at a fixed sampling cadence. It stores timestamps and aggregate activity rates, then exposes descriptive statistics (mean, max, deviation) and advanced measures such as the Hurst exponent via {\ttfamily \doxylink{classutil_1_1_hurst_estimator}{util.\+Hurst\+Estimator}}. Outputs are written to CSV or text summaries through export helpers.
